About The Portal

Built for The New College's Legacy of Excellence

The New College, Chennai, has stood as a beacon of higher education since 1951. This portal is an extension of that legacy — a digital infrastructure that upholds the same standards of fairness, rigour, and transparency that have defined generations of scholars from these halls.

Designed exclusively for internal assessments, the platform serves undergraduate and postgraduate students across all departments, giving faculty precise tools to gauge learning outcomes with confidence.

Fairness First

Every student receives a unique question order, reducing the chance of collaboration during the exam.

Secure Data Handling

All student data and responses are encrypted at rest and in transit following best practices.

Faculty Control

Department heads can create, schedule, and review assessments with granular permission controls.
